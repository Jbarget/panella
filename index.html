Our Story

Panella: Sicillian Street food is the creation of married couple Guiseppe and Caterina De Matteo. Hailing from Palermo [by way of Prato, Tuscany], they hope to bring you the finest in both Sicilian cuisine and hospitality. Reflecting the rich, varied heritage of this special place in the Mediterranean.

Learn More

Currently housed within the Goldfinger Factory underneath the iconic Trellick tower at 13-15 Goldborne Road, Panella invites you to head down for breakfast, lunch or and afternoon coffee. With a seasonal menu that changes from day to dat, they seek to reflect the very best of Sicilian and local British produce.
